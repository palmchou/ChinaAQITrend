Air Quality Trend in China

From 2008, the air quality in China had came to public attention and people were aware of its affection to heath. The Chinese government had enforced multiple polices trying to reduce the air pollution index since then. So here we are going to present the trend of the air quality in China and figure it out is the air pollution in cities in China reduced over years or became worse.
