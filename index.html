Air Quality Trend in China

From 2008, the air quality in China had came to public attention and people were aware of its affection to heath. The Chinese government had enforced multiple polices trying to reduce the air pollution index since then. So here we are going to present the trend of the air quality in China from 2014 to 2016 and figure it out is the air pollution in cities in China reduced over years or became worse.

Unlike other traditional common visualization topics like population and density, visualization of Air Quality is a new idea and it's meaningful to 1.4 billion people in China who may not aware the significance of the harmful pollution.
